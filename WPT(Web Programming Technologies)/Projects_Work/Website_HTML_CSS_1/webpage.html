Winter Trials : Students Cricket Association

The Student Cricket Association (SCA) is a vibrant and dynamic organization dedicated to promoting the sport of cricket among students. Our mission is to foster a love for cricket, develop skills, and encourage sportsmanship and teamwork.

  • Summer and Winter Camp Trials: Enhancing the cricketing skills of students.
  • Skill Development: Provide training sessions and workshops to enhance the cricketing skills of students.
  • Competitions: Organize inter-college and inter-school tournaments to offer competitive playing opportunities.
  • Community Building: Create a supportive community where students can share their passion for cricket and build lasting friendships
  • Health and Fitness: Promote physical fitness and a healthy lifestyle through regular practice and matches.
